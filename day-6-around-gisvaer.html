NORTHCAPE 2024

DAY 6 PM - BIRD SAFARI

The all-you-can-eat-buffet tourists from the Hurtigruten cruise arrive almost half an hour late. Having finally graced us with their presence, it’s off to an almost 2 hour boat trip during which we see a lot of flying objects of all sorts. Some of them also do some diving into the water. And some are already in the water. But they aren’t of the flying sort. They are seals.
