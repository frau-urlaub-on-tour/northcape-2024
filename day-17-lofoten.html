NORTHCAPE 2024

DAY 17 - LOFOTEN

Sandoya – Å – Sandoya (185 km)

The day starts very promising with blue skies and only patches of clouds. It continues like that on our drive down to the South of the Lofoten islands. It still looks ok-ish when we start our hike to the most famous Lofoten peak, the Reinebringen. And then….it all falls to pieces. Not in the wet way. But in the visual way. The fog is rolling in from the sea and decides to stay around the summit until we are back down at the car park. Now it has started raining. So we won’t want to go up again. What we do instead, is go to the famous Anita’s, a fish restaurant everyone recommends. And we don’t regret it. It’s pure heaven. Not only the fish. Also the desserts they have. If we were to have all we would like, we would need to climb Reinebringen at least another three times. Not today, tough. Brigitta and I might come back tomorrow after sending Julie off to the airport. And only for one round. We think.

Back home we enjoy a beautiful sunset. Even the sheep have climbed the rock to admire the spectacle.
