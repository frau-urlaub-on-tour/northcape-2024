NORTHCAPE 2024

CROSSING OVER TO SWEDEN

Berlin - Rostock – Gedser - Dragør – Klagsham (411 km)

Second leg – next change of plans. This time a bit more of a spontaneous one. Not being able to sleep before the “real” trip begins, I decide to get up again and get going at around 2.30 am. The run to Rostock is clear and at 5.30 am I arrive at the port. There is a 6 am ferry to Gedser, which is only a 2 hour crossing to Denmark instead of the 6 hour crossing directly to Sweden I had booked. But taking that booked one would mean another 6 hour wait till departure. So I decide to fork out the additional cash and off to Gedser it is.  However, it also means an extra 2 hour drive on the other side of the Baltic to my first destination in Sweden, Klagsham, just south of Malmø.

I stop for some pictures of the Øresundbridge from the Danish side in Dragør, then drive across it and check into my little studio with sauna. After stocking up on some provisions for the evening by car, off I go by bike to the bridge on the Swedish side. More pictures get taken. Then it's back home for some sauna time, and a quiet evening in.
