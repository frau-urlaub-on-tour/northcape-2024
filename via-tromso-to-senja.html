NORTHCAPE 2024

DAY 10 - VIA TROMSØ TO SENJA

Svensby – Senja (120 km, 2 ferry crossings)

We are up at 07.00 am and out before 09.00 am to catch the 09.30 am ferry to Breidvik. The drive to Tromsø is smooth, and at 11.15 am we pull up at Brigitta’s hotel. And now we are three! Norway nymphs on the go(go)!

It’s an equally smooth ride to the ferry port at Brensholmen where we have a nice 2,5 hour wait for our 03.00 pm ferry across to Senja. Time enough to have a little wander around the bay and to have a last minute grocery stock up.

Shortly after 04.00 pm we arrive in Husoy, a quaint little fishing village. We have a little place right by the water with stunning views across the fjord to the Segla and Hesten mountains. And even though it has started to cloud over, the atmosphere is mesmerizing.
